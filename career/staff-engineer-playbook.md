# Staff Engineer Playbook

Staff engineers scale systems AND people.

---

# 1. Scope

Senior:
Delivers features.

Staff:
Designs systems.

Principal:
Designs organizations.

---

# 2. Responsibilities

- Cross-team architecture alignment
- Risk assessment
- Long-term technical direction
- Mentoring seniors
- Writing ADRs
- Setting standards

---

# 3. Decision Framework

Ask:

- Does this scale?
- What fails under load?
- What happens in 2 years?
- What if team doubles?

---

# 4. Influence Model

Staff engineers influence through:

- Technical clarity
- Written communication
- Evidence (benchmarks)
- Calm crisis handling

Not authority.

---

# 5. Anti-Patterns

- Becoming the bottleneck
- Doing all the hard tasks
- Avoiding delegation
- Being the smartest in the room

---

# 6. Impact Measurement

Measured by:

- Reduced incidents
- Faster delivery
- Clearer architecture
- Stronger engineers

---

# 7. Final Rule

Staff engineers multiply capability.
